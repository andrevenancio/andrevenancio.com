Hangout Comedy Club


Client: Google
Agency: Toaster Ltd
Release: 2013
Role: Interactive Developer

Link: http://toaster.co/project/google-comic-relief-hangout-comedy-club

Google's Video Conferencing (Hangouts) and facial recognition technology detects how often you smile during a live comedy show to calculate how much you should donate to charity. Alongside Google Creative Lab, Parra & Potato London we designed and developed the YouTube Channel and “Laughometer” widget, which measures how much a laugh is worth, inviting fans to donate at the end of the show.
