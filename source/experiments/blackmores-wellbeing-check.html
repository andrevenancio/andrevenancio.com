Wellbeing Check


Client: Blackmores
Agency: Holler Australia
Release: 2016
Role: Interactive Developer

Link: wellbeingcheck.com.au

The Blackmores Wellbeing Check focuses on physical, mental and emotional wellbeing, presenting a series of questions to generate a personal 'wellbeing score' for each respondent. The wellbeing score provides a simple starting point on their wellbeing journey, empowering them with a greater understanding of where their health and wellbeing stands heading into 2016.
