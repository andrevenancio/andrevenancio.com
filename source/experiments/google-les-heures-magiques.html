Les Heures Magiques


Client: Google
Agency: 72andSunny
Release: 2015
Role: Tech Lead

Link: https://www.72andsunny.com/work/google/les-heures-magiques

Les Heures Magiques is a month long socially driven campaign that invites Parisians to rediscover their city at night. At the heart of the campaign is a mobile-first experience that showcases events that highlight the unexpected, forward-facing side of the city.
